Cookie Policy

When browsing the Internet, in some cases residual elements, such as temporary files, also called cookies, are stored on your computer or device. Each cookie is unique to your browser and contains specific anonymous information, such as web page identifier and name accompanied by ciphers and numbers.

These elements are routed to improve the user experience when browsing the web by allowing data retrieval on next visits and storage of your preferences to navigate more efficiently, quickly and simply.

TYPES OF COOKIES USED

Own:
are those sent to the user's end device, from an equipment or domain managed by the publisher itself, and by the one providing the service requested by the user.

Third Party: are those sent to the user's end device, from a device or domain whose management is not done by the publisher, but by another entity responsible for the data obtained through cookies.

Session: are cookies designed to obtain and store data while the user accesses a web page.

Persistent: are cookies in which the data remains stored on the device and can be accessed and processed for a period of time determined by the party responsible for the cookie. The period extends from a few minutes to many years.

Analysis: are those treated by us and third parties, which allow us to quantify the number of users and thus carry out the measurement and statistical analysis of the use of users of the service offered. To this end, we analyze your navigation on our website in order to improve the products or services we are offering.

Advertising: when you visit web pages or open emails in which advertisements or promotions are published about our products or services, a cookie will be installed in your browser, which will serve for us to later display advertising associated with your searches and develop ad tracking related to, for example, the number of times they are seen, location where they appear, at what time, etc.
